Your Child's Next School in Asia

Cognita is a global education group operating 11 international schools across Singapore, Hong Kong, Thailand, Vietnam and India. Our schools offer internationally recognised curricula including the IB, British, American and Australian pathways, from early years through to Grade 12.

How we support relocating families

  • Admissions teams experienced in supporting families relocating to Asia at short notice
  • Mid-year and mid-term enrolment available - students can join within weeks of applying
  • Multiple curriculum pathways to match your child's previous schooling and future goals
  • English language support available for students who need it
  • Schools across five countries, giving families genuine choice of city, lifestyle and educational approach
  • A global school network across Asia, Europe and the UK, so if your family moves again, transitions between Cognita schools are straightforward

Already at a Cognita school?

Transferring within the Cognita network is designed to be seamless:

  • No new application form - our admissions teams handle the paperwork on your behalf
  • Assessments minimised or waived, with school reports accepted in place of formal testing
  • Application and enrolment fees waived
  • Tuition fees already paid are prorated and transferred to your new school
  • A dedicated admissions concierge coordinates directly between your current and new school
